What is Climate Change

Climate Change is the changing of the Earth's weather and temperature patterns. This can be a natural occurance, due to the activity of the sun and different volcanic activity. But... since the 1800's, humans have been the biggest cause of the change in the Earth's climate

Reference: United Nations 'What Is Climate Change?' Available at: https://www.un.org/en/climatechange/what-is-climate-change [Accessed 5 December 2024]



What is the purpose of our website?

The purpose of our website is to help inform you about climate change, and multiple different aspects to do with climate change, while providing easy to implement into your daily life solutions, to help combat climate change once and for all. We will also inform you of targets that have been set by the United Nations, which we're hoping to contribute to. You will learn about the consequences of climate change, dealing with those consequences and future preventative measures.

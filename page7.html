Consequences of Climate Change

About Climate Change and its Impacts


According to articles such as, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Welcome to United Nations, The threats that humans will face due to climate change are rising sea levels which will increase the possibility of flooding, rising temperatures which can cause droughts and in return will lead to an increase of hunger and lack of food nutrition. With the lack of food, not only will humans have to deal with this exhausting impact, but animals alike will also have trouble with the lack of food which can cause extinction of the most common animals like squirrels. Not only would our hunger strike, but human health will also significantly decrease due to air pollution. Climate change can also cause extreme weather events to increase.

Increasing Sea Levels
Climate Change affects our seas and with the possibility of increasing sea levels, this can cause excessive floods in areas you would not expect. This will endanger certain species in the arctic, causing endangerment for humans and animals.

Rising Temperatures
The threats that humans will face due to climate change can be the rising temperatures which can cause droughts, especially in the warmer climates. This will also affect the food distribution system and farms.

Lack of food resources
The lack of food resources is caused by floods, droughts and decrease of farm animals. This would impact both animals and humans. For animals, it would result in a smaller food chain, which could unfortunately cause certain species to go extinct, and they would have to adapt to their environment. While we humans need constant production of food to keep clean and healthy diets to survive.
